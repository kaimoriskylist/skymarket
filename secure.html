Secure Trading Guide

This guide explains how to trade safely on Sky Market. Please read carefully before making any transaction.

1. Always Use the Middleman

  • Never trade directly with unknown users.
  • Use Sky Market’s official middleman service.
  • Middleman ensures both buyer and seller are protected.

2. Verify Official Contacts

  • Only trust contacts listed on the Sky Market website.
  • Fake staff and impersonators are common.
  • If unsure, ask for verification in the Discord server.

3. Payment Safety

  • Sky Market uses crypto-only payments.
  • Never send funds before middleman confirmation.
  • Double-check wallet addresses.

4. Account Delivery

  • Accounts must be delivered through the middleman.
  • Verify access before confirming completion.
  • Change all credentials immediately after receiving.

5. Common Scams to Avoid

  • Users asking to skip middleman.
  • Fake Discord servers or copied usernames.
  • Pressure tactics or limited-time threats.
⚠️ If anything feels suspicious, stop immediately and contact Sky Market staff through the official Discord server.
